HAPPY NEW YEAR!!

Hello Kooky !

Knowing you has been the greatest gift of my life.
I cherish every moment we’ve spent together, and those yet to arrive.
Memories of our laughter, arguments, and even the tears we’ve shared are treasures I hold dear.
Being honest, I never thought we’d come this far, yet you've made it all clear.
Even so, I’m grateful that you’ve stood by my side, unwavering and strong.
Remember that no matter what, I’ll always be here, where you belong.
Life may not have given us ideal circumstances right now, but I believe in us.
You are my partner, my strength, and my dream until it last.

Choosing to make this work is a commitment I wholeheartedly embrace.
As the year ends, I dream of spending the next one with you—and many more in decades.
My wish is to share every celebration with you, not just from afar but together.
And if it’s within God’s plan, I hope we can share a lifetime, even 100 years, stronger.
Nothing would mean more to me than to hold you close and feel your warmth today.
Given the distance, though, I know this longing must wait and stay.
Yet, I’m hopeful that next year will bring us closer in every way.
All we need is to focus on ourselves, growing stronger and better, we'll pray.
Next year-end, may we celebrate this love in person, together, as we’ve always dreamed.

Because with you, my love, everything is as perfect as it seemed.


I'm sorry if it's short compared to my previous letters, but I poured a lot of my thoughts and feelings in creating this letter/poem if you will, even if its short, and I'm tired of always reminiscing about the past. I want to focus on the present and hope that I will still be with you in the future. I want to greet you again a Happy New Year!!! and I love you always💜💜
HAPPY NEW YEAR!!
I LOVE YOU SO MUCH
- SEANNY
